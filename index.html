Vision

At AcumenAI, we strive to empower both store owners and suppliers to have more control over how buyers interact with their products.

Mission

Our mission is to bring e-commerce metrics to brick-and-mortar stores, seamlessly integrating with existing surveillance infrastructure.

Increase customer loyalty through Anonymized Gait Recognition

We, at AcumenAI, hold privacy to high standards. Instead of using
intrusive facial recognition, we have developed our own in-house gait
recognition models, that can accurately recognize returning customers in your stores.
By integrating with existing surveillance infrastructure, we keep ourc gosts low, allowing even for small stores to easily adopt our solution. Say goodbye to loyalty cards!

Services

We help you transform your store into the store of the future!

Video Behavioral Analytics

We use the existing surveillance infrastructure to analyze the behaviour of customers through our custom AI models. We offer fine grained analytics about customer-product interactions.

Customer re-identification

Returning customers are identified based on their walking style with our in-house algorithm, to allow for better informed remarketing strategies on purchasing habits.

Online Metrics to the real world

Reach, impressions and engagements are now possible by studying the real-world customer responses to in-store ads.

Privacy by Design

Our gathered data about users is anonymized and fully compliant with current regulations.
